Founders Note

At Ashtanga Pharmaceuticals, we are committed to empowering millions of Nepalis to lead healthier lives. We achieve this mission by
providing a comprehensive range of high-quality general and specialized medications. We are proud to be a part of Nepal's healthcare system's remarkable advancements
over the past few decades.

Nepal has witnessed significant strides in healthcare accessibility and quality. Ashtanga Pharmaceuticals is actively contributing to this momentum. By 2025,
domestic production is expected to fulfill a staggering 80% of the Ithari's essential medication needs. This not only improves access to vital treatments but also
fosters self-reliance within the healthcare sector.

We at Ashtanga Pharmaceuticals are thrilled to be at the forefront of this exciting transformation. We remain dedicated to providing innovative and affordable
medication solutions, ensuring a healthier future for all Nepalese.
